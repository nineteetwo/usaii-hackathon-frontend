Emergency Contacts

Tap a number below for more information or to call immediately.

911 General Emergency

Call 911 for life-threatening emergencies, crimes in progress, or immediate medical assistance. Do not use for non-emergencies.

Call 911
988 Suicide & Crisis

The 988 Lifeline provides 24/7, free and confidential support for people in distress, prevention and crisis resources for you or your loved ones.

Call 988
Poison Control

Expert advice for poisoning emergencies and questions. Available 24/7. Call if you suspect a poisoning has occurred.

Call 1-800-222-1222
311 Non-Emergency

Use 311 to find information about services, make complaints, or report problems like graffiti or road damage to your local government.

Call 311
Domestic Violence

National Domestic Violence Hotline. Essential tools and support to help survivors of domestic violence so they can live their lives free of abuse.

Call 1-800-799-7233
SAMHSA Helpline

Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration. Free, confidential, 24/7 treatment referral and information service.

Call 1-800-662-4357
